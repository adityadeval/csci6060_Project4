Australia, officially the Commonwealth of Australia, is a sovereign country comprising the mainland of the Australian continent, the island of Tasmania, and numerous smaller islands. Australia is the largest country by area in Oceania and the world's sixth-largest country. Australia is the oldest, flattest, and driest inhabited continent, with the least fertile soils. It is a megadiverse country, and its size gives it a wide variety of landscapes and climates, with deserts in the centre, tropical rainforests in the north-east, tropical savannas in the north, and mountain ranges in the south-east.

Places to Visit:
Sydney: Explore iconic landmarks such as the Sydney Opera House and Sydney Harbour Bridge. Relax on Bondi Beach, stroll through the Royal Botanic Garden, and visit the historic Rocks area.
Great Barrier Reef: Discover one of the world's natural wonders with snorkeling or diving adventures among vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life.
Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park: Witness the awe-inspiring Uluru (Ayers Rock), a sacred Aboriginal site, and explore the striking rock formations of Kata Tjuta (The Olgas).

Currency and Exchange Rate:
The currency of Australia is the Australian Dollar (AUD).
1 Australian Dollar (AUD) = 0.71 US Dollars (USD).

Hotels:
Australia offers a wide range of accommodation options, from luxury resorts and boutique hotels to budget-friendly hostels and holiday parks.
In major cities like Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane, you'll find a variety of hotels catering to different budgets and preferences.
